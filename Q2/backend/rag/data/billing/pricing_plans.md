## Account Management

### Upgrading Your Plan
1. Log in to your account dashboard
2. Go to "Billing & Plans"
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the upgrade

Changes take effect immediately, and you'll be prorated for the current billing period.

### Downgrading Your Plan
1. Contact support to initiate downgrade
2. Data backup recommended before downgrading
3. Changes take effect at next billing cycle

### Cancellation Policy
- **Monthly Plans**: Cancel anytime, no penalties
- **Annual Plans**: Cancel with 30 days notice
- **Enterprise Plans**: Per contract terms

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I change my plan anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my usage limits?
You'll receive notifications when approaching limits. Overages are charged at standard rates unless you upgrade your plan.

### Do you offer discounts for non-profits?
Yes, we offer 25% discounts for qualified non-profit organizations. Contact sales for details.

### Can I pay annually?
Yes, annual billing is available for Basic and Professional plans with a 15% discount.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise customers.

### Is there a setup fee?
No, there are no setup fees for any of our plans.

### Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Monthly plans can be canceled immediately, while annual plans require 30 days notice.

### Do you provide invoices?
Yes, invoices are automatically generated and sent via email. You can also download them from your account dashboard.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, allowing you to reactivate your account. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted.

### Can I get a custom plan?
Yes, we offer custom Enterprise plans tailored to your specific needs. Contact our sales team for details.
